# Google Analytics E-Commerce Dashboard | Power BI
### Portfolio Project — Oyewo Lukman Segun

## Overview
4-page interactive Power BI dashboard analysing 12 months of 
e-commerce Google Analytics data.

**Dataset:** 437,596 sessions | $1.25M revenue | Jul 2024–Jun 2025

## Dashboard Pages
| Page | Content |
|------|---------|
| Traffic Overview | 438K sessions, bounce rate, new vs returning |
| Acquisition | 7 channels, $1.4M revenue, conversion rates |
| Revenue & Products | 10 products, heat map, category donut |
| Geography & Devices | 10 countries, device split, bounce rate |

## Key Insights
- Email converts at 6.22% — highest despite lowest volume
- Q4 (Oct–Dec) shows clear seasonal revenue peak
- Desktop users convert at 2x the rate of mobile users
- United States drives 38% of total revenue

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop | DAX | Conditional Formatting | Bookmark Navigation
